Background

  • Pathogens are organisms that cause disease
  • Common plant pathogens include fungi, bacteria, viruses, and nematodes
  • They can spread through soil, water, air, and human activity
  • Specific nematodes target Almond Trees
  • This leads to loss in gross income in Merced County
